Meet Amati Simmons

Lead Designer · Founder · Toledo, Ohio

Amati Simmons is the lead designer and founder of J&J Branding Co., a graphic arts and media management studio based in Toledo, Ohio. With more than 10 years of experience in digital design, branding, and web solutions, Amati brings both artistry and strategy to every project.

J&J Branding Co. was established in 2019 with a clear focus: support small business owners and entrepreneurs with visuals and digital systems that feel as intentional as the brands behind them. From launch-day logos to full website overhauls, Amati walks clients through each phase with clarity and care.

As a Canva, Wix, and AWS-certified partner, Amati blends user-friendly tools with powerful infrastructure—creating designs that not only look beautiful but also perform reliably behind the scenes.
